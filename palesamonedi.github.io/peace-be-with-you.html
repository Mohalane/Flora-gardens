Arrangement Flower Care by Fabulous Flowers

Follow these simple care guidelines to keep your Fabulous Flowers arrangement vibrant and fresh. Keep the oasis at the base of the arrangement moist by adding 10ml of water between the flowers every 1-2 days. Focus the water around the centre of the oasis while keeping the surrounding box dry. Place your flower arrangement in a cool, dry location, away from direct sunlight, fires, or radiators, for maximum longevity. Once the flowers have lived their life, repurpose the box as a keepsake or for storage.
